<p><p>The high-voltage bushings market encompasses three main types: composite bushings, compound-filled bushings, and condenser bushings. Composite bushings are lightweight and offer excellent dielectric properties, making them suitable for harsh environments. Compound-filled bushings use an insulating compound to enhance performance and reliability, particularly in outdoor applications. Condenser bushings feature integrated capacitors, allowing for precise voltage measurement and control, primarily in transformer applications. These bushings are critical for ensuring safe and efficient electrical power transmission.</p></p>
