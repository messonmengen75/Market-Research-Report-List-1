<p><p>Engraving and milling machines for the ceramic market are classified into two main types: Three-Axis and Five-Axis machines. Three-Axis machines operate along X, Y, and Z axes, allowing for straightforward engraving and milling tasks on flat surfaces. Conversely, Five-Axis machines offer enhanced capabilities by adding rotation about the X and Y axes, enabling more complex geometries and precise detailing on intricate shapes. Both types cater to diverse ceramic applications, including decorative designs and precision part manufacturing.</p></p>
