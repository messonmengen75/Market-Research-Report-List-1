<p><p>The aluminum metal fencing market is primarily divided into two types: mesh and strip. Mesh fencing features lightweight, interconnected aluminum wires that create a flexible and open barrier, ideal for security and visibility. It is often used in agricultural, commercial, and residential applications. Strip fencing, on the other hand, consists of solid aluminum strips, providing a more solid and decorative option. It offers enhanced privacy and aesthetic appeal, making it suitable for residential areas and gated communities. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
