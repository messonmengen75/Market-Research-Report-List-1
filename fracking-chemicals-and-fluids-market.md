<p><ul><li>Water-Based</li><li>Oil-Gel Based</li><li>Foam-Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fracking chemicals and fluids market comprises three main types: water-based, oil-gel based, and foam-based fluids. Water-based fluids are the most common, utilizing water mixed with additives to improve flow and reduce friction. Oil-gel based fluids use oil as a base, offering enhanced viscosity and stability in high-temperature conditions. Foam-based fluids combine gas and liquid, creating a lighter, less invasive alternative that minimizes water use and environmental impact, making them suitable for specific geological formations.</p></p>
