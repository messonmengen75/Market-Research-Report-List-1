<p><p>The Triiodothyronine (T3) test kit market includes various methodologies such as Time-resolved Immunofluorescence (TRIF) and Chemiluminescence Immunoassay (CLIA). TRIF employs fluorescent labels and specialized time-resolved detection to enhance sensitivity, making it suitable for low-concentration samples. In contrast, CLIA utilizes luminescent reactions for quantifying T3 levels, offering rapid results and high throughput. Both methods cater to healthcare settings for diagnosing thyroid disorders, each with unique advantages in sensitivity, speed, and ease of use, contributing to the growing demand in the market.</p></p>
