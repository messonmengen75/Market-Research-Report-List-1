<p><p>The direct-to-consumer genetic sequencing market encompasses several types, including diagnostic screening, preimplantation genetic diagnosis (PGD), and relationship testing. Diagnostic screening involves assessing individuals for genetic predispositions to health conditions. PGD allows prospective parents to screen embryos for genetic disorders before implantation during in vitro fertilization. Relationship testing provides insights into genealogical ties, paternity, or ancestry. Each type leverages advancements in genetic technology to empower consumers with personalized health and ancestry information, enhancing informed decision-making in healthcare and familial connections.</p></p>
