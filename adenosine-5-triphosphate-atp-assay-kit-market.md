<p><p>The ATP Assay Kit market encompasses three primary types: Luminometric, Colorimetric, and Fluorimetric ATP Assay Kits. Luminometric ATP assays detect light produced by ATP in a bioluminescent reaction, offering high sensitivity. Colorimetric assays measure changes in color intensity related to ATP concentration, providing a straightforward and cost-effective method. Fluorimetric assays utilize fluorescent dyes that bind to ATP, allowing for sensitive detection via fluorescence measurement. Together, these kits cater to various research and industrial applications, enabling effective ATP quantification.</p></p>
