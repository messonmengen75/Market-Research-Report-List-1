<p><p>Self service systems are categorized into two main market types: on-premises and cloud-based. On-premises systems require installation and maintenance on the user's local hardware, providing greater control and security but demanding higher upfront costs and IT resources. Cloud-based systems, on the other hand, are hosted online, offering flexibility, scalability, and lower initial expenses through subscription models. This allows organizations to easily access and manage services remotely, ensuring quicker updates and reduced maintenance efforts.</p></p>
