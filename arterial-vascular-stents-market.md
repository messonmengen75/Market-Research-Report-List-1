<p><strong>The Arterial Vascular Stents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self Expansion</li><li>Balloon Dilatation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The arterial vascular stents market is primarily categorized into two types: self-expanding stents and balloon dilatation stents. Self-expanding stents automatically expand upon deployment, providing consistent support for arterial walls, making them ideal for complex lesions. In contrast, balloon dilatation stents require a balloon to be inflated for expansion, effectively widening narrowed vessels. This differentiation allows for tailored treatment approaches based on patient needs, lesion characteristics, and procedural preferences, thereby enhancing overall vascular health outcomes.</p></p>
