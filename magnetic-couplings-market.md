<p><ul><li>Disc-type Coupling</li><li>Synchronous Coupling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The magnetic couplings market includes two primary types: disc-type coupling and synchronous coupling. Disc-type couplings utilize a set of magnets arranged in a circular pattern, allowing for torque transmission while minimizing backlash and wear. They are ideal for applications requiring precise alignment and low maintenance. In contrast, synchronous couplings rely on a magnetic field to synchronize the movement of drive components, ensuring accurate timing and minimal vibration in dynamic systems. Both types enhance efficiency and reliability in various industrial applications.</p></p>
