<p><ul><li>Optical Line Terminal (OLT)</li><li>Optical Network Terminal (ONT)</li><li>Passive Optical Splitters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Gigabit Passive Optical Network (GPON) equipment market comprises several key components: Optical Line Terminals (OLTs), Optical Network Terminals (ONTs), and passive optical splitters. OLTs serve as the central hub, connecting to the internet and managing multiple ONTs in homes or businesses. ONTs act as the end-user device, converting optical signals into electrical ones for local use. Passive optical splitters distribute the optical signals from the OLT to multiple ONTs, enabling efficient bandwidth sharing over long distances without power consumption.</p></p>
