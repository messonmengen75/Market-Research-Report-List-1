<p><p>The flexible screen market is primarily divided into PMOLED (Passive Matrix OLED) and AMOLED (Active Matrix OLED) technologies. PMOLED displays, favored for smaller screens, are simpler and cost-effective but have limitations in resolution and size. In contrast, AMOLED offers superior image quality, faster refresh rates, and more vibrant colors, making it ideal for larger and high-definition displays. As consumer demand for innovative and curved screens rises, the market for both technologies continues to expand, catering to diverse applications in electronics.</p></p>
