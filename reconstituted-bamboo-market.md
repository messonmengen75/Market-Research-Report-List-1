<p><p>The reconstituted bamboo market features two primary types: cold pressing and hot pressing. Cold pressing involves compressing bamboo fibers at lower temperatures, preserving natural colors and textures, resulting in a denser, more environmentally friendly product. In contrast, hot pressing uses heat and pressure to bond bamboo particles, creating a smoother finish and enhancing durability, making it suitable for various applications, including furniture and flooring. Both methods cater to diverse consumer preferences and sustainability demands in the market.</p></p>
