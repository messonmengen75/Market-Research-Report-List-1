<p><p>The swimming pool chemical market comprises several key types essential for maintaining water quality. Beaching powder is a strong oxidizer, effectively disinfecting and whitening pool water. Sodium hypochlorite serves as a powerful liquid chlorine alternative, providing convenient sanitization. Liquid chlorine offers a more stable and concentrated form of chlorine for easy application. Trichloroisocyanuric acid (TCCA) is a popular chlorine stabilizer, slowly releasing chlorine while resisting degradation from sunlight. Other market types may include algaecides and flocculants, contributing to overall water clarity and safety.</p></p>
