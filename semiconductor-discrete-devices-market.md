<p><p>The semiconductor discrete devices market consists of various components crucial for electronic applications. Diodes/rectifiers control current flow and convert AC to DC. Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistors (IGBTs) offer high efficiency in power applications, while Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Field-Effect Transistors (MOSFETs) are widely used for switching and amplification. Bipolar Junction Transistors (BJTs) provide reliable current amplification, and thyristors are utilized for high-voltage, high-current applications in power control. Together, these devices support diverse industries, enhancing energy efficiency and performance.</p></p>
