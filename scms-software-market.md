<p><p>Supply Chain Management Software (SCMS) is categorized into cloud-based and on-premise markets. Cloud-based SCMS is hosted on remote servers, offering scalability, accessibility, and lower upfront costs, as users can access it via the internet. In contrast, on-premise SCMS is installed locally on a company’s servers, providing greater control and customization, but typically involves higher initial investments and maintenance responsibilities. Each type serves different business needs, balancing flexibility and control versus cost and convenience.</p></p>
