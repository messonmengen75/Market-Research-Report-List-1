<p><ul><li>Outbound DNS</li><li>Inbound DNS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The DNS security tools market is divided into outbound and inbound DNS segments. Outbound DNS focuses on securing queries from internal networks to prevent data leaks and malicious responses, ensuring safe internet access. In contrast, inbound DNS protects external queries directed to an organization's servers, safeguarding against threats like DDoS attacks and domain hijacking. Together, these segments enhance overall cybersecurity by ensuring both the integrity of outgoing requests and the safety of incoming traffic to network resources.</p></p>
