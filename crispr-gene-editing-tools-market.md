<p><p>The CRISPR gene editing tools market comprises several key components. Cas nucleases serve as precision cutting enzymes for DNA. Guide RNA directs these nucleases to specific genomic locations, ensuring targeted editing. DNA ligases facilitate the joining of DNA fragments post-editing, enhancing the efficiency of the modification process. Restriction endonucleases are included as traditional tools that also enable precise DNA cutting, complementing CRISPR. Other market types encompass additional tools and technologies that support or enhance CRISPR applications in research and therapeutics.</p></p>
