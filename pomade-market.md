<p><strong>The Pomade Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-based Pomade</li><li>Oil-based Pomade</li><li>Concrete class Pomade</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pomade market is categorized into three main types: water-based, oil-based, and concrete class pomades. Water-based pomades are easy to wash out, offer a lighter hold, and are ideal for a natural finish. Oil-based pomades provide a stronger hold and added shine but are harder to rinse out. Concrete class pomades, known for their extreme hold, are designed for elaborate styles and long-lasting effects. Each type serves different styling preferences and hair types, appealing to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
