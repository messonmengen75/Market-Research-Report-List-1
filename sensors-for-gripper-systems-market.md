<p><p>The sensors for gripper systems market comprises various types, including piezoelectric sensors, torque sensors, and others. Piezoelectric sensors are utilized for detecting force and pressure changes, providing precise feedback for gripping applications. Torque sensors measure the rotational force applied by the grippers, ensuring optimal handling without damaging objects. Other sensors may include strain gauges and proximity sensors, which enhance functionality and safety by monitoring grip strength and object detection. Together, these sensors improve the efficiency and reliability of robotic gripping systems.</p></p>
