<p><ul><li>PET</li><li>PVC</li><li>Vinyl Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automobile wrapping film market consists of various materials, primarily PET, PVC, and vinyl resin. PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) offers durability and resistance to environmental factors, making it suitable for high-performance applications. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is cost-effective and widely used due to its flexibility and ease of application, though it may have limitations in longevity. Vinyl resin films are known for their high color fidelity and conformability, ideal for intricate designs and custom wraps, enhancing both aesthetics and protection for vehicles.</p></p>
