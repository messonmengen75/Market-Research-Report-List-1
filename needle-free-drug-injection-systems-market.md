<p><p>Needle-free drug injection systems encompass various types designed for painless and efficient drug delivery. Jet-based injectors use high-pressure jets to penetrate the skin and deliver medication. Spring-based injectors utilize mechanical springs to rapidly deliver the dose through the skin. Laser-powered injectors employ focused laser energy to create openings in the skin for drug delivery. Vibration-based injectors use rapid vibrations to facilitate drug penetration. Each type offers unique advantages in comfort, speed, and application versatility, improving patient adherence and reducing fear of needles.</p></p>
