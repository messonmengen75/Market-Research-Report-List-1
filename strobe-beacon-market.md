<p><strong>The Strobe Beacon Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Gas Strobe Beacon"</li><li>"LED Strobe Beacon"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Strobe Beacon market is primarily divided into two types: Gas Strobe Beacons and LED Strobe Beacons. Gas Strobe Beacons utilize gas-discharge technology to produce light, typically offering high-intensity illumination and a wide coverage area, making them suitable for outdoor applications. In contrast, LED Strobe Beacons employ light-emitting diodes for illumination, offering energy efficiency, longer lifespan, and reduced maintenance. Both types serve critical roles in safety signaling across various industries, such as transportation, emergency response, and construction.</p></p>
