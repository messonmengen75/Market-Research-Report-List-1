<p><p>The animation collectibles market consists of various types, each appealing to different collectors. Original Production Art includes unique, hand-drawn pieces used in animated films, offering authenticity and a direct connection to the creators. Reproduction Art features prints or replicas of original works, making them more accessible but less unique. Concept Art showcases the initial ideas and designs for characters and settings, providing insight into the creative process. Storyboards represent key scenes and sequences, documenting the narrative flow of animated projects.</p></p>
