Market types include fence detection, which uses sensors along barriers; IR beam detection, utilizing infrared technology to identify breaches; and ground detection, employing sensors buried in the soil. Security topping involves protective barriers on fences, while temporary/portable detection solutions offer flexibility for events or changing sites. IP system integration allows for seamless connectivity with surveillance networks, and other systems cover niche technologies, enhancing comprehensive perimeter security.</p></p>
