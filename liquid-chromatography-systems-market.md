<p><p>The Liquid Chromatography Systems market includes various types, primarily Liquid-Liquid Chromatography (LLC) and Liquid-Solid Chromatography (LSC). LLC involves the separation of compounds based on their solubility in two immiscible liquids, enabling efficient purification of pharmaceuticals and environmental samples. In contrast, LSC utilizes a solid stationary phase with liquid mobile phase, focusing on separating mixtures based on differences in adsorption. Both methods are vital in analytical chemistry, biochemistry, and industrial applications for effective component analysis and purification processes.</p></p>
