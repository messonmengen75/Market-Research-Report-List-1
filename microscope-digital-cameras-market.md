<p><ul><li>CMOS</li><li>CCD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microscope digital cameras market primarily includes two types: CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) and CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) sensors. CMOS cameras are favored for their lower power consumption, faster frame rates, and cost-effectiveness, making them ideal for routine applications. Conversely, CCD cameras are known for their superior image quality and lower noise levels, often preferred in high-end scientific and medical applications. Both types cater to diverse needs across research, education, and industrial sectors, enhancing imaging capabilities in microscopy.</p></p>
