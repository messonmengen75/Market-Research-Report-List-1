<p><p>The multi-functional commercial printer market is primarily divided into laser and inkjet types. Laser printers use toner and are known for high-speed printing, precision, and efficiency, making them ideal for large volume tasks and professional environments. In contrast, inkjet printers utilize liquid ink and excel in producing high-quality color prints and images, which makes them suitable for graphics-heavy applications and smaller print runs. Both types cater to diverse business needs, combining printing, scanning, copying, and faxing capabilities.</p></p>
