<p><p>The automotive rear view camera modules market primarily includes two types of camera technologies: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD cameras offer superior image quality and high sensitivity, making them suitable for low-light conditions, while CMOS cameras are more energy-efficient, compact, and cost-effective. Both technologies enhance vehicle safety by providing drivers with a clear view of obstacles behind the vehicle, thereby reducing the risk of accidents during reversing and improving overall driving convenience.</p></p>
