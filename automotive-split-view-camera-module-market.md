<p><p>The Automotive Split-view Camera Module market is primarily segmented into two types based on sensor technology: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) chips and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) chips. CCD chips are known for superior image quality and low noise levels, making them ideal for high-performance applications. In contrast, CMOS chips offer lower power consumption, faster processing speeds, and integration capabilities, leading to increased popularity in modern vehicles. Both types enhance driver visibility and safety through advanced camera systems.</p></p>
