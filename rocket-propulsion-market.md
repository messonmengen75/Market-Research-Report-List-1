<p><strong>The Rocket Propulsion Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid Propulsion</li><li>Liquid Propulsion</li><li>Hybrid Propulsion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rocket propulsion market comprises three main types: solid propulsion, liquid propulsion, and hybrid propulsion. Solid propulsion utilizes a solid fuel and oxidizer mixture, offering simplicity and reliability but limited thrust control. Liquid propulsion involves separate fuel and oxidizer, providing adjustable thrust and higher efficiency, often used in larger rockets. Hybrid propulsion combines both solid and liquid elements, balancing efficiency and simplicity, allowing for adjustable thrust while maintaining the advantages of solid fuel. Each type serves distinct applications in space exploration and satellite launches.</p></p>
