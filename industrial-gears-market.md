<p><p>The industrial gears market encompasses a variety of types, each serving specific applications. Spur gears offer straightforward motion transfer and are widely used due to their simplicity. Helical gears provide smoother and quieter operation, ideal for high-speed applications. Gear racks convert rotational motion to linear motion, essential in machinery. Worm gears allow for high torque in compact spaces. Bevel gears enable the change of axis in motion. Other gear types include planetary and double helical gears, catering to various industrial needs.</p></p>
