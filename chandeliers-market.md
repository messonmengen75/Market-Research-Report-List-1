<p><p>The chandelier market encompasses various styles catering to diverse lighting needs and aesthetics. Uplight chandeliers project light upwards, enhancing room ambiance. Pendant chandeliers hang from ceilings and serve as artistic focal points. Each type serves unique decorative and practical purposes.</p></p>
