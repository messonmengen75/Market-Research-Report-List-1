<p><p>The bottled water testing market encompasses various types, each targeting different aspects of water quality. Microbiological testing assesses the presence of harmful microorganisms, ensuring safety for consumption. Physical testing evaluates attributes like color, odor, and turbidity, impacting aesthetics and consumer perception. Chemical testing focuses on contaminants, nutrient levels, and pH, influencing health and safety standards. Together, these testing types ensure bottled water is safe and compliant.</p></p>
